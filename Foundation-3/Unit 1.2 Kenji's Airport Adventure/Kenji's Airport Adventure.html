Kenji's Airport Adventure

A story about traveling and making quick decisions.

Scene 1: Security Check

Kenji is at the airport. He is going to visit his grandparents. He puts his bag on the conveyor belt. He takes out his keys with a cool keychain and puts them in the tray.

He checks his pockets. He has some gum, some cash, and a receipt from a souvenir shop.

Scene 1: Security Check

The security officer waits. Suddenly, Kenji remembers. In his backpack, he has a full water bottle! He can't take it through security.

Class Decision: What should Kenji do?

PATH A

Scene 2: A Kind Stranger

Kenji throws the water bottle away and passes security. While collecting his things, he sees a woman drop her purse. Her things spill out.

PATH A

Scene 2: A Kind Stranger

Kenji helps her. He sees a hairbrush, a mirror, and some hand lotion. The woman is very thankful. "You are very kind," she says. "Please, take this candy bar."

Class Decision: What should Kenji do?

PATH A - ENDING

Scene 3: At the Gate

Kenji arrives at his gate. The woman is there! She is sitting next to his grandparents. She is his grandmother's old friend! They all laugh. Whether he took the candy or not, his act of kindness made the start of his trip a happy one. He sits down, ready for his flight.

THE END

PATH B

Scene 2: A Dizzy Spell

Kenji steps out of line and drinks the whole water bottle. He feels very full and a little dizzy. He gets through security and sits on a bench to recover.

PATH B

Scene 2: A Dizzy Spell

As he stands up, his wallet falls! His driver's license falls out. A young man picks it up. "Be careful," the man says, handing it back. "You dropped this." He also offers Kenji some tissues.

Class Decision: What should Kenji do?

PATH B - ENDING

Scene 3: At the Gate

Kenji arrives at his gate and sees the same young man. He is also on the same flight! They start talking. The man is visiting his cousin in the same city. Because Kenji was friendly, he made a new acquaintance for the flight. He sits down, ready for his trip with a new friend.

THE END
