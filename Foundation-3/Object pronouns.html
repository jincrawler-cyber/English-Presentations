🚫 Repetition sounds bad:

I have a new friend named John.

I like John very much.

I call John every day.

It sounds repetitive, right?

We can make it better!

✅ Use "Helper Words" (Pronouns):

I have a new friend named John.

I like him very much.

I call him every day.

Him = John

Look at the examples from your book

• He tells me he doesn't want anything.

• I usually get him something boring.

• I hope he likes it.

• I never know what gift to get her.

• She never likes them (perfume or clothes).

• They usually buy us books.

Let's analyze these words!
