A Saturday with Kenji

An interactive story to learn vocabulary about relationships.

Key Vocabulary: Relationships

Family

  • Parents
  • Siblings
  • Grandparents
  • Uncle / Aunt
  • Cousin
  • Niece / Nephew

Romantic

  • Boyfriend / Girlfriend
  • Fiancé / Fiancée
  • Crush
  • Ex
  • Situationship

Friendship

  • Best friend
  • Close friend
  • Acquaintance
  • Confidant

Professional

  • Colleague / Co-worker
  • Boss / Manager
  • Partner

Social

  • Neighbor
  • Peer
  • Teammate, Classmate

Scene 1: The Morning

It is Saturday. Kenji is in his apartment. He is a student. His neighbor, Mrs. Gable, is an old, friendly woman. Kenji looks at his phone.

Scene 1: The Morning

He has a message from his best friend, Yumi. Yumi is his confidant. But then, he sees another message from Haru. Haru is Kenji's crush. Some classmates and Haru are going to go to the park.

Class Break:

  • Who is Yumi? (Yumi is Kenji's best friend.)
  • What is Haru to Kenji? (Haru is his crush.)

Scene 2: The Decision

Kenji decides to be responsible. He texts Haru: "I can't. I have to study."

He calls Yumi: "Yes, I am going to meet you at the library."

Scene 2: The Decision

Yumi says: "Great! My cousin, Akari, is in the city. Can she come? She is a peer; we are the same age."

On his way out, he sees Leo, the nephew of his neighbor. Leo is waiting for his aunt.

Class Break:

  • Who is Akari? (She is Yumi's cousin.)
  • Who is Leo? (He is the nephew of Kenji's neighbor.)

Scene 3: At the Library

At the library, Yumi says: "Kenji, this is Akari. He's my close friend."

Suddenly, Kenji sees Sara, his ex. They are now just acquaintances. She is with a man.

Scene 3: At the Library

Sara says: "Kenji! Hi! This is my fiancé, David." David asks if they were classmates.

Kenji tells Yumi: "No problem. She has her life, I have my life."

Class Break:

  • Who is Sara? (She is Kenji's ex.)
  • Who is David? (He is Sara's fiancé.)

Scene 4: A Call From Work

Kenji works part-time at a café. His phone rings. It's his boss, Mr. Tanaka.

Mr. Tanaka says: "My partner in the business is sick. And your colleague, Maria, has a family emergency. Can you work?"

Scene 4: A Call From Work

Kenji tells his friends: "My manager needs me at work. My teammate can't go."

Akari says: "You should go. We are a good team." Kenji is going to leave.

Class Break:

  • Who called Kenji? (His boss / manager.)
  • Why does he need to work? (Because his colleague has an emergency.)

Scene 5: The End of the Day

Kenji finishes his shift. He helped his boss and his co-worker.

He sees Haru at the park entrance. Haru is alone. Haru says: "I only invited the others because I wanted to see you."

Scene 5: The End of the Day

Kenji is surprised. Maybe this is not a situationship, but a real connection.

Kenji asks: "Are you free tomorrow evening?" Haru says yes. Kenji says: "Great. I am going to call you."

It was a day full of different people: his best friend, his ex, his boss, and now... maybe his future girlfriend.

THE END
