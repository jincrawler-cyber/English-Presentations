The Rule Fixed Arrangements

We can use the Present Continuous for 100% confirmed future plans (arrangements). You must include a future time word!

1. The Grammar (Same as always)

Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

Example: They are flying...

2. Future Time Expressions ⏰

tonight tomorrow next weekend on Saturday in December
💡

Usually, these plans involve other people, buying tickets, or making reservations.

🗣️ Let's Talk!

Work in pairs. Ask your partner about their real plans for the future. Use the formula: "What are you doing...?"

Student A asks:

  • 👉 What are you doing tonight?
  • 👉 What are you doing tomorrow morning?
  • 👉 What are you doing this weekend?
  • 👉 What are you doing next summer?

Student B answers:

Use your real plans!

  • > I am watching a movie tonight.
  • > I am staying at home.
  • > I am playing videogames.
  • > I don't have plans yet! (Si no hay plan)
