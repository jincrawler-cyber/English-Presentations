Rule #1 Present Simple

We use it for routines, habits, and permanent facts.

Structure

I / You / We / They work

He / She / It works

Signal Words 🚦

always usually sometimes every day
"I drink coffee every morning."

Rule #2 Present Continuous

We use it for actions happening right now or temporary situations.

Structure (BE + ing)

I am eating

He/She/It is eating

We/You/They are eating

Signal Words 🚦

now right now at the moment today
"Look! The cat is drinking your coffee right now!"
