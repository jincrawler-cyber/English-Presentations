Rules & Facts: IF vs WHEN

1. Look and Compare

What tense are the verbs in?

Possibility

IF...

  • If you forget your passport, you miss your flight.
  • If it rains, we visit the museum.
Certainty

WHEN...

  • When the plane lands, you unbuckle your seatbelt.
  • When you cross the border, you show your ID.

All verbs are in the...

PRESENT SIMPLE

3. The Difference

IF

50%

A Possibility

It might happen. We use it for emergencies, bad luck, or alternatives.

"If you lose your ticket..."

WHEN

100%

A Certainty

It is guaranteed to happen. It is just a matter of time during the trip.
